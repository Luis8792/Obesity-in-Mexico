Exercises to lose weight

  • Walking: It is effective and beneficial for health. It is walking forcefully using the arms.
  • Body-walking: The optimal thing is to reach a speed that maintains the heart rate at levels appropriate for the person's age.
  • Power-Walking: It is a variant of walking in which the effort is significantly intensified. It is a particularly vigorous and light gait. Without elements that increase the effort.
  • Wogging: It is a variant of walking, it is about increasing the effort with the help of weight, for example, weighted wristbands.
  • Hill-Wogging: It involves walking uphill, but at a faster walking pace, which in combination with the steepness of the terrain subjects the circulation to a not excessive effort.
  • Race-walking: More extreme variant of walking, in which you walk at maximum speed.
  • Bench Press, Pull-Up, Deadlift, and Squat.
